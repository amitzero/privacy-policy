Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

At LINKWALL, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and protection for all users, especially children. Our app strictly prohibits any form of content, behavior, or activity related to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitment

We have implemented comprehensive measures to prevent, detect, and respond to any instances of CSAE, including:

  • Strict moderation policies to prevent inappropriate content.
  • Advanced monitoring tools to identify and remove harmful material.
  • Collaboration with law enforcement and child protection organizations.
  • Clear reporting mechanisms for users to flag inappropriate behavior or content.

Reporting and Action

If you encounter any content or behavior related to CSAE on our app, please report it immediately through our reporting page. Our team will take prompt action, including removing content, banning users, and notifying authorities as required by law.

External Standards

LINKWALL adheres to internationally recognized standards and guidelines to combat CSAE, including:

  • WePROTECT Global Alliance
  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • INHOPE: International Association of Internet Hotlines
