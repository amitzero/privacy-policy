Privacy Policy - ZeCon

ZeCon is a secure messaging app that allows users to communicate using email-based authentication.

This app is designed to provide a private and secure messaging experience.

By using this app, you agree that:

  • Internet access is required for sending and receiving messages.
  • We do not store or access your messages beyond what is necessary for delivery.
  • End-to-end encryption is used to protect your conversations.
  • The camera is used to scan contact QR codes and capture images.
  • Images from the camera and gallery can be sent through the app.
  • Notifications are used to alert users about new messages.

We do not collect any personal information beyond what is required for authentication.

We do not share any information with third parties.

We do not use any information for any other purpose.
