Safety Standards - ZeCon

At ZeCon, we prioritize the safety and security of our users. Our app is designed with robust measures to ensure a safe messaging experience.

Our Commitment

We have implemented comprehensive measures to prevent, detect, and respond to any instances of abuse or exploitation, including:

  • Strict moderation policies to prevent inappropriate content.
  • Advanced monitoring tools to identify and remove harmful material.
  • Collaboration with law enforcement and child protection organizations.
  • Clear reporting mechanisms for users to flag inappropriate behavior or content.

Reporting and Action

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates our safety standards, please report it immediately through our support email. Our team will take prompt action, including removing content, banning users, and notifying authorities as required by law.
